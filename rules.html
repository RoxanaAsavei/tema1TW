rules
At any point during the game, you should have these in mind:
  1. On a turn, a player rolls the die and moves one of their tokens forward the number of spaces indicated by the die.
  2. A token can only move forward onto an empty space or onto a space occupied by an opponent's token, which sends the opponent's token back to its starting area.
  3. If a player rolls a 6, and they have tokens in house they are forced to move one of those tokens. Otherwise, they can move a token that is already in game.
  4. If a player cannot move any of their tokens, their turn is forfeited.
  5. Tokens must travel around the board once before entering the player's home area.
  6. Tokens enter the home area when they reach the center of the white square. To enter, the player must roll the exact number needed.
